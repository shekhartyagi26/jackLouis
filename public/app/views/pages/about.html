SVP Apparels Pvt Ltd., based in Pune, Maharashtra is the registered owner, exclusive manufacturer and marketer of the brands ‘Jack Louis ™ & Fitful ®. The company exclusively manufactures and markets brands like Jack Louis™, Jack Louis Kiddos™, Jack Louis for Her™, Fitful®.
The company was set up in September 2017 with a vision to create strong apparel brand with innovative products for Men, Women and Kids. With intent to create innovative products at most affordable prices, the company has been striving to create a niche in Comfort Wear and Active Wear Apparels.
The company has its Corporate Office at Pune, Maharashtra with manufacturing and warehousing facility at Navsari, Gujarat. Company’s management & Board of Directors consists professionals from various educational background and have vast experience working in various Multinational Companies in different industries.
Since its inception in September 2017, company has created its distribution network in various states in India such as Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana& Karnataka. The company has planned to expand its distribution network in the states of Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, & Chattisgarh.
